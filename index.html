Welcome to Pollution Awareness

Understanding the causes, effects, and prevention of Air, Water, and Sound Pollution.

Air Pollution

Air pollution occurs when harmful substances such as gases, dust, or fumes are introduced into the atmosphere.

  • Causes: Vehicle emissions, industrial discharge, burning of fossil fuels.
  • Effects: Respiratory problems, global warming, acid rain.
  • Prevention: Use public transport, plant trees, use renewable energy.

Water Pollution

Contamination of water bodies due to human activities like dumping waste and chemicals.

  • Causes: Industrial waste, sewage discharge, plastic pollution.
  • Effects: Marine life destruction, unsafe drinking water, diseases.
  • Prevention: Proper waste disposal, reduce plastic use, clean-up programs.

Sound Pollution

Unwanted or excessive noise that affects human and animal life.

  • Causes: Traffic, loudspeakers, industrial noise.
  • Effects: Hearing loss, stress, sleep disturbance.
  • Prevention: Use silencers, avoid honking, create quiet zones.
